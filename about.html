About Me

I was born and raised in Mesa, Arizona and am the daughter of my amazing parents, Manuel and Benita. As a Chicana or Mexican American, I strive to make the best of every opportunity to make my parents proud of me. I finished my Associates in Applied Science for Web Development in Summer 2019 and am continuing my studies at ASU to obtain my Bachelors Degree in Web Development by Fall 2021. Before completing my Associates degree, I had very little knowledge of coding and design but what inspired me to learn something new was an acupuncture session. I was lying on a table with needles in my back and when the therapist asked what made me happy, I couldn't answer. I took this as a sign to reflect on my previous job experiences and realized I needed to go back to school to do something that would challenge me. I've always dreamed of working remotely and traveling the world and I decided coding was the path I would take to achieve this. When I'm not learning about web design, my hobbies include drawing, watching Korean Dramas and playing videogames.
